Instructions on how to play go here...

  • Press any key to begin.
  • Simon will play a sequence of colors and sounds.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking on the buttons in the same order.
  • If you repeat correctly, Simon adds a new color to the sequence.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends.
  • Press any key to play again.
  • Try to repeat as many sequences as possible to beat your previous high score.
